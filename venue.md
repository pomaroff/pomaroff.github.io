## Where

The entire day will take place at The Riverstone Estate to make the most of this stunning vineyard.

We're sure you'll love the venue as much as we do, perched on top of a hill with a near 360-degree view of the surrounding Yarra Valley in all of its Autumn colour.

We can't wait! <i class="fas fa-grin-beam icon style4"></i>
